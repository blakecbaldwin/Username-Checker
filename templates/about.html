About Username Scan

Username Scan is a tool designed to help you quickly check if a username is available across multiple popular platforms. Whether you're a content creator, brand, or gamer, finding a consistent name is important—and that's where we come in.

What We Do

  • Check username availability on sites like YouTube, Twitch, TikTok, GitHub, and more.
  • Show results clearly with links to existing profiles when usernames are taken.
  • Validate usernames with accurate pattern checks per platform.

Why Use This?

Instead of manually searching each site, use Username Scan to get results all in one place—fast and simple.

Note

This is an unofficial tool and not affiliated with any of the platforms it checks.
