Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy outlines how we handle your information when using Username Scan.

Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data or usernames submitted through this site. All checks are performed in real-time and are not saved.

Cookies & Tracking

Username Scan does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not analyze, track, or log any usage behavior beyond basic server access logs.

Third-Party Services

Our tool interacts with public websites to check usernames. We are not affiliated with any third-party services and do not share data with them.

Security

We take basic security measures to ensure your connection is safe, but this site is intended for general use only and should not be used for sensitive or secure data.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Please check this page for the latest version.
